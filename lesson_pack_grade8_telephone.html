Listen to the Conversation
Identify polite words & expressions

Listen carefully to the telephone conversation between James and Mary. Notice the words highlighted in gold — these are the polite expressions used.

📞 James – Caller
📱 Mary – Receiver
🐢 Speed:
Tap Play to begin.
📞
James – Caller
Hello, good morning. This is James speaking. May I please speak to Mary?
📱
Mary – Receiver
Good morning, James! Mary speaking. How are you?
📞
James – Caller
I am well, thank you for asking. I am calling because I would like to enquire about the human rights meeting, if you don't mind.
📱
Mary – Receiver
Of course, not at all. The meeting is on Friday at two o'clock. Would that be convenient for you?
📞
James – Caller
Yes, Friday works well. Thank you very much. Could you kindly send me the venue details as well?
📱
Mary – Receiver
Certainly! I will send them shortly. Is there anything else I can help you with?
📞
James – Caller
No, that will be all. Thank you so much for your time. I really appreciate your help.
📱
Mary – Receiver
It is my pleasure. Have a wonderful day. Goodbye!
📞
James – Caller
Goodbye, Mary. Take care. Thank you once again!

💡
Tips for Your Role-Play
  • 👋Always greet the person and introduce yourself first.
  • 🗣Speak clearly and at a steady pace — not too fast.
  • 🙏Use polite words: please, thank you, may I, certainly, of course.
  • 👂Listen without interrupting and wait for your turn to speak.
  • 👋End the call politely: Goodbye, take care, thank you for your time.
